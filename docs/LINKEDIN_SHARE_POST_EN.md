Incoming supplier invoices in SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition projects often still start with a shared mailbox, PDF checks, and manual mapping validation.

I wrote an article about a small SAP BTP pattern we built to make this process more automatic, traceable, and clean-core friendly.

The solution reads invoice emails, extracts PDF invoice data, checks supplier/BP, company code, tax code, and GL mappings, and prepares the SAP supplier invoice payload. Missing mappings go to manual review instead of creating wrong SAP records.

The article also covers the S/4HANA Cloud Launchpad setup, including Custom Tile, Launchpad Space/Page, Business Role, and Business User assignment, plus the customer questionnaire for Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace scenarios.
